Why Become a Partner?

%WEB%

Compete on “Time to Value” instead of “Price"

IT is getting crushed by an overwhelming influx of app demands. Price is always important but it is no longer a sufficient differentiator, time to value is worth far more to your customer. OutSystems Platform dramatically improves the economics of custom app development by 3-4x for both time to value and resource utilization giving our partners a competitive advantage. We enable you to enter a new account more quickly and capture a larger share of their IT spend due to these improved economics.

%/WEB%
%WEB%

Increase recurring revenue streams with all your customers

A recurring revenue stream is an obvious goal, but how? OutSystems Platform dramatically improves the productivity of the entire lifecycle of an app while enabling greater predictability in terms of costs to provide feature enhancements and maintenance. Subscription services are a natural extension to project-based offerings. The opportunity for recurring revenue is found not only in the license subscription for OutSystems Platform but also in managed services you can provide to your clients.

%/WEB%
%WEB%

Improve productivity and resource utilization to increase profit

In today's project-based app development market, a broad set of specialized skills are required. These skills are often spread across a variety of team members and can present bottlenecks or gaps. OutSystems Platform allows your strongest and most skilled developers to build reuseable components that are easily accessible to all other developers. This improves the level of productivty of your entire staff and tackles the bottlenecks. This increase in productivity results in more predictable delivery and client satisfaction as well as increased profitability on your projects.

%/WEB%
%WEB%

Leverage the Platform to broaden your addressable market

Many of our partners leverage OutSystems Platform to expand their services portfolio. With the Platform, management consulting firms can now be competitive with off-shore shops; BPM or BI consultancies can now build a general purpose, custom app dev practices; and application modernization practices can build competency around a newer generation plaform to carry them for the next decade or more. In all these cases, these firms are leveraging the power of the Platform to overcome barriers to entry to these close proximity services opportunities.
