Extend SaaS and on premises systems

Accelerate integration with existing cloud or legacy systems using pre-built adapters, or create your own. Use connectors to fetch and update Salesforce.com information, easily access SAP data, connect to existing databases, integrate with web services and read and transform files. You can deploy your connectors once and reuse them over and over across your applications.

Stop customizing your systems of record

Create a public, private or hybrid cloud architecture to deploy applications that keep you away from changing core systems - which are not built to change. Create an architectural layer of services that can be reused in applications that need to be flexible. You will dramatically improve your time to market, lower the cost of maintenance for your legacy systems, reduce your technical debt, and have the ability to quickly react to changes.

A unified interface across your distinct systems

Integrate services, data and cross-system processes to deliver a consistent view across your existing systems. Mash up information from your on-premises ERP, SaaS packages, or legacy systems. Deploy highly usable applications, in the cloud or your data center, that are easy to continuously change with no impact to your backend systems.

Expose your core system information in a secure and scalable way

Making your on premises core systems information accessible on public portals (either in the cloud or in your own data center) poses serious security and performance issues. With the OutSystems Platform, you can build highly transactional portals using asynchronous jobs and integration workflows to handle all the caching and high processing requirements, while keeping your core system safe from heavy load and security threats.
