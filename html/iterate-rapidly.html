Iterate Rapidly, Without Risk

OutSystems Platform was built from the ground up to ensure fast and secure change during the whole life cycle of your applications.

You can make deep changes to your applications' data model, APIs and architecture, knowing that all dependencies will be automatically updated.

No matter how much you modify your applications, OutSystems Platform acts as a co-pilot and gatekeeper to guarantee zero-error deployment.

Continuous integration and change with zero risk

Change applications faster, decrease maintenance costs, and ensure that no matter how big the change, your applications will always be architecturally sound and functional. OutSystems Platform tracks global dependencies and pinpoints the impact of a change across all layers of your application. It heals the sections that can be automatically corrected and tells you about the required manual corrections. It also automates the deployment process including all database change scripts and the impact this change will have in the running application.
